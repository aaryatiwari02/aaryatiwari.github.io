Problem Statement

The e-commerce platform experienced a significant drop in conversion rates, with users abandoning their carts at a higher rate than usual. This negatively impacted sales and overall revenue.

Technical Issues

  • Page load times increased by 2 seconds on average
  • Mobile users experienced frequent checkout errors
  • Payment gateway integration issues reported
  • Inconsistent product information displayed

Business Impact

  • Conversion rates decreased by 20%
  • Cart abandonment rate increased by 30%
  • Customer satisfaction scores dropped by 15%
  • Potential revenue loss of $10K per week

My Role & Responsibilities

Student Analyst & Case Study

I conducted a root cause analysis as a case study to identify and understand the factors contributing to the decline in conversion rates on the e-commerce platform.

Data Analysis

Analyzed website traffic, user behavior, and system logs to identify potential causes

Cross-functional Research

Researched best practices and consulted with online resources to gather insights

Solution Exploration

Proposed potential solutions based on analysis and research findings

Report Presentation

Presented findings and recommendations in a comprehensive report

Approach / Thought Process

1

Data Collection & Monitoring

Set up comprehensive monitoring and collected 30 days of system logs, performance metrics, and user activity data to establish baseline patterns.

2

Pattern Analysis

Used statistical analysis and visualization tools to identify correlations between system load, processing times, and failure patterns.

3

Hypothesis Formation

Developed multiple hypotheses about potential root causes including database bottlenecks, memory leaks, and inefficient algorithms.

4

Systematic Testing

Conducted controlled experiments and load testing to validate hypotheses and isolate the primary contributing factors.

Execution & Collaboration

Led a cross-functional investigation team of 8 members across engineering, DevOps, and data teams to systematically identify and resolve performance issues.

Retrospective

What Went Well

  • Systematic approach led to quick identification of root causes
  • Strong cross-team collaboration and knowledge sharing
  • Comprehensive monitoring setup provided valuable insights
  • Phased implementation minimized customer impact
  • Exceeded all performance improvement targets

Challenges & Learnings

  • Initial data collection took longer due to incomplete logging
  • Some performance issues were intermittent and hard to reproduce
  • Coordinating testing windows with customer usage patterns
  • Balancing quick fixes vs. long-term architectural improvements

Future Improvements

  • Implement proactive monitoring and alerting systems
  • Establish regular performance review processes
  • Create runbooks for common performance issues
  • Invest in automated testing for performance regressions
