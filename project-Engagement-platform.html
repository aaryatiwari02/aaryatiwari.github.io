Problem Statement

The existing communication setup was fragmented, channel-specific, and operated without a unified orchestration layer while upholding GDPR Compliance practices. This created gaps in visibility and control, as campaign analytics were dependent on the third-party tool Clevertap, limiting in-platform reporting capabilities. As a result, customer experiences lacked transparency, personalization opportunities were restricted, and integration efforts were high due to the need for repetitive configurations between both systems.

System Issues

  • Analytics outside the platform: Reliance on Clevertap for reporting leads to limited in-product visibility, broken drill-downs, and delayed insights.
  • Inconsistent event tracking: Metrics and events are defined differently across channels/tools, making end-to-end attribution unreliable.
  • No unified consent & preferences: Opt-ins, digests, and channel priorities aren’t enforced centrally, risking over-messaging or compliance gaps.
  • High integration overhead: Provider setup, credentials, templates, and webhooks duplicated per channel/brand with brittle configs. Routine changes (templates, audiences, schedules) require multi-tool edits and manual coordination.

Business Impact

  • Inconsistent brand voice across channels
  • Difficulty in measuring combined impact of campaigns
  • Higher operational cost due to siloed workflows
